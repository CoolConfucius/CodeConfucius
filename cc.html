Programming Projects:

  • Calculator in jQuery

    Does addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division operations. Can insert decimal numbers and toggle between positive and negative numbers. Allows chain operations.

  • Towers of Hanoi Game in jQuery

    Begins with three stacks: the first one full with ordered disks and the other two empty. User can only move the top disk of a stack to the top of another stack. No disk can be placed on top of a smaller disk. User wins by moving all the disks to the third stack. Has a reset button. Can toggle the difficulty level (number of disks to play with).

  • Single Player Blackjack 21 against AI in jQuery

    User plays against an AI with a standard deck of 52 cards. Face cards count as ten points. Player and AI can count an ace as 1 point or 11 points. Other cards are counted as their numeric value. Both players begin with two cards. User can choose to “hit”, add a card to their hand, or “stand”, take no cards. The player whose hand exceeds 21 is busted. When neither busts, the player with higher points win.

  • Two Player Tic Tac Toe in jQuery

    In a 3x3 grid, two users, O and X, take turns marking their letter on the grid by clicking. The user who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins.

  • Single Player Battleship against AI in jQuery

    Two 10x10 grids. User places 5 ships of lengths—2, 3, 3, 4, 5 squares—onto their grid. May place the ships horizontally or vertically. On the other grid, the AI has 5 ships of the same lengths placed randomly. All ships must be within their grids and not overlap. AI’s ships are hidden from the user. User and AI take turns hitting a square on each other’s grid. When a ship is hit, the square is marked as hit. Else, the square is marked as miss. The player who sinks all their opponent’s ships wins.

  • Animal Shelter App in Mean Stack

    Switch among pages through Angular UI Routing. Can read, add, update, and remove clients and animals in the database. Clients can adopt and unadopt animals.

  • Bank App in Mongo DB, ExpressJS, Jade, and Passport

    Users can create accounts and log in. Passwords are secured with hash. Can withdraw from and deposit into, and transfer between checking and savings balances. Can view a transactions log that shows the amount, description, and date of each transaction.
